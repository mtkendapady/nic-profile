Here’s a little something about me…

Raised along the Wasatch Front, I love the outdoors, disc golfing, my wife and dog, and of course, programming. Not necessarily in that order. I've been writing iOS apps since May 2015 and graduated DevMountain's immersive iOS cohort on August 5, 2016.

I work for Areios where I function as both project manager and lead developer for a team of skilled iOS developers in a dynamic environment. Projects are always done on time with as little tech debt as possible. During the day I also teach at DevMountain as a way of giving back to the community and enabling others who want to better their lives by joining the industry.
